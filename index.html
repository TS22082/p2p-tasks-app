Welcome!

This is an app you can use to keep track of todos items! When you create your profile I (the developer) have no access to the data you create, but if you want to share your todo items, you just have to give the person you want to share the dat key that is generated for you. They will only be able to view it when you have the app open. Bonus: If you want to change the UI or add cool new features. . . Do it! You, as the user, own the experience.
